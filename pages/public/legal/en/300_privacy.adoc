ifeval::[{cookies} == true]
[role="mt-5"]
== Cookies

This website uses cookies and similar technologies to work properly. You
can accept, refuse, or change your choices at any time using the privacy
settings dialog.

To open it later, click the cookie icon
mdi:cookie[24px, md-blue] in the top-right of the *menu bar*.

[role="mb-4"]
We only use *advanced* third-party cookies if you give us your permission.
These extra cookies help us analyse how the site is used or show you
content that fits your interests. They make your visit smoother and unlock
extra features. You can read the full details by clicking *Privacy Policy*
in the cookie dialog or in the sections below for each provider.

Necessary::
Necessary cookies store the information needed for the basic features of
this site. We do not keep records of how you use them. All this data goes
into so-called *session cookies*, which your browser *deletes automatically*
when you close all open tabs.

Analyses::
Analytics cookies help us understand how people use this site so we can
make it better. We use Google Analytics (GA) for this. We do not send any
personal data, and IP addresses are *anonymised* to protect your privacy.

Personalization::
Personalization cookies help us collect feedback, offer extra features, or
run advertising campaigns. The income from advertising lets us keep this
website free for you. Our partners help us provide a better personal
experience and cover the costs of running the site.
endif::[]

ifeval::[{themes} == true]
[role="mt-5"]
== Bootstrap Themes

Bootstrap themes change how a website looks while keeping the same
features. A theme can change colours, fonts, font sizes, and the look of
elements like buttons, forms, or menus. It does not change what these
elements do.

The built-in *Themer* module uses the free Bootstrap themes from
_Bootswatch_. Bootswatch offers many ready-made themes that work
directly with websites built on Bootstrap V5.

The Bootswatch service uses *permanent* cookies to remember which theme
you picked. So, to use the themes feature in J1 Template, you need to
accept *permanent* cookies for *personalization*.
endif::[]


ifeval::[{logs-files} == true]
[role="mt-5"]
== Log files

Our web servers automatically collect some information and save it in
log files. This information may include your IP address, browser type,
internet provider, the page you came from, the page you went to next,
your operating system, the time of your visit, and similar details.

The data we save includes:

* Browser type and version
* Operating system
* URLs you visited
* Hostnames
* Time stamps for each page

We may combine this log data with other information. We do this to
improve our services and the content on this site.
endif::[]
