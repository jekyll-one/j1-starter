[role="mt-5"]
== Discuss with Respect

The conversation in the comments section is one of the best parts of a
blog. Sometimes, though, comments can turn negative or fill up with spam.
That is why we have this comment policy. It explains what we accept and
what we do not, so everyone knows what to expect.

[role="mt-4"]
=== Comments are Welcome

We welcome and encourage comments on this site. In some cases, however,
we will edit or remove a comment. The owner of this site can edit or
delete any comment at any time, without giving notice.

[NOTE]
====
This comment policy may change at any time.
====

[role="mt-5 mb-7"]
Promotional::
We will delete comments that are spam or only there to promote something.
You can include a link if it is helpful, but the comment itself should be
about the article.

Profanity::
We will delete comments that contain swear words.

Rough language::
We will delete comments with language or ideas that may offend other
readers. This includes anything abusive, threatening, sexual, misleading,
or that damages someone's reputation.

Attack::
We will delete comments that attack any *person* directly.

Harassing::
We will delete comments that harass other readers. We welcome different
opinions, but every comment must stay respectful toward our authors and
other commenters.

Anonymous::
We will delete anonymous comments. We only accept comments from people
who give their name.
